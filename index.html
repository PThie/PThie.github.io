about

I am currently a Data Scientist and Ph.D. student in Economics in Essen, Germany. I work at the Research Data Center of the RWI Leibniz Institute for Economic Research as a researcher focusing on housing markets, urban economics, and the evaluation of environmental factors. I am passionate about dealing with all kinds of data - the more, the better. I work with R but also use Python to tackle data challenges.

My motto: Don't stand still. I am always keen to learn new tools. So, I am also currently diving into HTML, SQL, and the world of data pipelines.

I presented my work at several international conferences. We might have already met.
